Describe a View or Table

If your model includes multiple tables and views, you may find it convenient to add descriptive notes or comments to your model.

There are two ways to identify the tables and views.

Provide a descriptive name for each view and table by double clicking its name in the table browser and entering a new name.

Add identifying notes in the bottom pane of the table browser. Just select a view or table, click in the bottom pane and enter your remarks. Each time you select that view or table, your notes appear to remind you what is displayed in it. These notes are just for reference; they can't be printed.
